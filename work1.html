Pickup App

A service to help people pick up garbage and earn tips at the same time

Overview

This is a project I personally initiated to help Shanghai residents solve the problem of garbage recycling difficulties. I started this project independently and was responsible for the project's research, interface design, user testing and high-fidelity prototype production.

Role

UI/UX Designer, Project Management

Timeline

3 months

Tools Used

Sketch

BACKGROUND

On July 1st, 2019, Shanghai officially implemented new regulations on the management of domestic waste. After this implementation, the community began to remove garbage bins, merged them into several garbage drop-off points. Many communities set these two time periods to throw garbage: 7:00-9:00 and 18:00-20:00. This means numerous office workers who leave early and return late have difficulties throwing trash away during the workday.

Shanghai is one of the most densely populated cities in China, with the vast majority of people living in apartments or connected houses. Whether or not we can use the diverse structure of the community to solve the dilemma of office workers has sparked my interest.
